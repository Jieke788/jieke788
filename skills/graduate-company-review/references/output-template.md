# Output Template

Use this structure for final answers. Keep it concise, evidence-based, and readable for a graduating student.

## Recommended structure

### 1. 公司概览

Include:

- company name
- target role if given
- target city if given
- one-line headline judgment

### 2. 主营业务与现状

Cover:

- what the company does
- how it likely makes money
- market or industry position if visible
- any meaningful current-state signal such as expansion, layoffs, or funding pressure

Clearly tag claims as `事实` or `推断` where needed.

### 3. 岗位薪资观察

Cover:

- likely graduate roles related to the request
- salary range or average signal by role and city when evidence exists
- whether the sample is solid or thin

If the evidence is weak, write `样本不足`.

### 4. 优势

List the strongest reasons a new graduate might still consider the company.

### 5. 风险

List the clearest downside signals first. If risk is material, do not bury it after minor positives.

### 6. 综合评分卡

Use this format:

- 薪资竞争力: X/10
- 成长空间: X/10
- 业务前景: X/10
- 稳定性: X/10
- 工作强度风险: X/10
- 管理与口碑风险: X/10
- 校招友好度: X/10
- 岗位匹配度: X/10

### 7. 结论

Use exactly one of:

- `适合`
- `谨慎`
- `不推荐`

Then explain the reason in 2 to 4 short sentences.

### 8. 信息来源

List the main sources as Markdown links when available. Prefer named sources over raw domains.

### 9. 置信度

Use one of:

- `高`
- `中`
- `低`

Then explain why the confidence is at that level.

## Style rules

- Default to Chinese.
- Write for a new graduate making a real decision.
- Avoid dramatic language.
- Do not overfit to one salary screenshot or one complaint thread.
- If the company is not clearly good, make the caution legible.
